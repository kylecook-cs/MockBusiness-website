About Us

Cook’s Beer Café is owned and operated
by Kyle Cook and friends. The doors opened
on December 10th, 2017, welcoming beer
enthusiasts and casual social drinkers alike.
Our goal is to pass on our knowledge and
love for great tasting beers to our friends in
our community.

The bar has 20 rotating taps of domestic and
imported beer, which are hand selected to
bring the best experience to our customers.
Cook’s Beer Café also serves bottled and
canned beer from all over the world that can
be enjoyed in house or taken to go to be enjoyed later.

they can be depended upon to meet any national crisis.
The great point is to bring them the real facts, and beer.”
